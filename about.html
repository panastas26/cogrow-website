Our Story

CoGrow started with a simple observation: professional service firms and field service companies spend a fortune generating leads — but most of them can't respond fast enough to convert them.

We watched CPA firms lose $30K clients because the intake call came in at 6 PM. We saw pest control companies pay $80 per click only to send the caller to voicemail. We talked to law firms spending $20K+ a month on lead gen with a 14-hour average response time.

The data was clear: 78% of customers buy from the company that responds first. And yet most businesses were responding in hours or days, not minutes.

So we built CoGrow — purpose-built AI systems that respond to every lead instantly, qualify them automatically, and book them on your calendar before the competition even picks up the phone.
