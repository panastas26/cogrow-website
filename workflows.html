construction What we build

Inside a bigger team,
here's what AI takes off your plate.

Picture a 100-person HVAC shop. Same repetitive jobs eat the week. Here's the kind of work we put AI on.

trending_up

We write up your estimates

A tech walks the job, gives the details, and AI builds the estimate. Your guys spend less time at a keyboard and more time on the next call.

receipt_long

We turn job photos and notes into the paperwork

Photos from the truck and a few notes become the invoice, the work order, and the warranty record. No retyping at the end of the day.

forum

New leads get a fast reply

A call comes in or a form gets filled out and the right person hears about it right away. The customer gets a real answer fast, and nothing sits overnight.

dashboard

One weekly report that tells you what happened

Every Monday, a plain-English summary of the week: jobs done, money in, where things got stuck. You see it without chasing anyone for numbers.

psychology

We capture what your senior people know

Your best tech has fixes in his head that nobody wrote down. We pull that knowledge into something the whole crew can ask, so it doesn't walk out the door when he retires.

school

We train your team to use AI on the job

Buying the tools is the easy part. We sit with your people and show them how to actually use AI in the work they do every day, so it sticks.
