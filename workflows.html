The work between your tools

Every business runs on 5–10 SaaS tools. CRMs, accounting platforms, communication tools, BI dashboards, calendars. They don't talk to each other.

So instead of running your business, you spend your week stitching data together by hand — pulling reports, copy-pasting between tabs, rebuilding the same dashboards, drafting the same emails. Hours every week. Sometimes a full day.

That's the work that should be automated. Not replaced — automated. So the work still gets done, sounds like you, and stops eating your week.
