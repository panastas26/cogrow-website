Resources

We help small and mid-sized businesses put AI to work. Here is how we help, plus guides you can read for free.

Two ways we help

Most businesses come to us for one of two things. Here is the plain version of each.

travel_explore

Get Found & Get Booked

If customers can't find you online, we fix that. We build your website, set up your Google listing, and make sure new leads get a fast reply, so people find you first and book you.

precision_manufacturing

Put AI to Work Inside the Business

For bigger teams, we put AI on the repetitive jobs that eat your week: estimating, paperwork, reporting, capturing what your veterans know, and training people on the job.

A few examples

These are illustrations of the kinds of problems we help with, not specific client results.

call_missed

A home-service shop missing calls

A small crew is out on jobs all day, so calls go to voicemail and the caller dials the next company. A fast auto-reply by text can catch that caller, ask what they need, and get them on the calendar before they move on.

support_agent

A professional office buried in intake

A firm gets more inquiries than its front desk can handle, and good leads wait hours for a reply. An assistant that answers right away, gathers the basics, and books the right ones for a call frees the team to do the actual work.

description

A growing team stuck on paperwork

As a company grows, the same forms, estimates, and reports get rebuilt by hand every week. Putting AI on those repeat jobs gives the team back hours and keeps the steady knowledge of long-time staff from walking out the door.
